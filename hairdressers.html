Since 1956

Since 1956 we have been styling people's hair in many different ways. Overtime our company has developed into a famous barbers, hairdressers and salon. Our founder Arthur is very proud of what his company has become. His plan was to create a place where everyone, man, woman or child could visit for a new look. This plan has totally succeeded!
