Since 1956

Since 1956 we have been styling peoples hair in many different ways. Overtime our company has developed into a world famous barbers, hairdressers and salon. Our founder Arthur would be proud of what his company has become, though sadly he died of cancer in 2002. His plan was to create a place where averyone, man, woman, child or elderly could visit for a new look.
