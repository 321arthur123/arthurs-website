We are a small restaurant in Sudbury who specialise in burgers. The burgers we make are the BEST you will ever taste. These burgers are made by our professional chefs, who cook to make your day great! We believe in making the best burger for you, so we will make any of your requests happen. We will even change the music to your prefered choice! Also we prioritize in making your experience at Burger Bar as comfortable as possible so we have private booths to ensure you get the privacy you deserve!
